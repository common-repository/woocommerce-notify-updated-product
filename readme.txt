Keep your customers in the loop with real-time updates on their favorite WooCommerce products!

== Description ==

Enhance your customer's shopping experience with 'WooCommerce Notify Updated Product'! Whenever you make an update to a product in your WooCommerce store, don't let your customers miss out. This essential plugin ensures that all your customers are promptly informed via email about the latest updates. Perfect for digital content sellers, this tool keeps your audience engaged and informed about the newest versions of your products, enhancing satisfaction and loyalty.

Product info: [eggemplo](https://www.eggemplo.com/plugin/woocommerce-notify-updated-product)

== Installation ==

Add the new plugin you have received and activate it:

Go to Plugins > Add New > Upload and click the Browse... button to select the plugin's zip file, usually woo-notify-updated-product-x.y.z.zip (x, y and z are the plugin's current version numbers).

After that proceed to activate the plugin.

If you have errors, review the plugin's page located here: http://www.eggemplo.com/plugins/woocommerce-notify-updated-product for similar problems and the proposed solutions posted in the comments.
